About

Zaamom, born in Morocco, was greatly inspired by art from a young age. He grew up in the Ocean neighborhood of Rabat. Seawater became ink for his drawings and waves, a source of inspiration for his work as well as a playground for his favorite sport: bodysurfing. After studying contemporary literature, Zaamom attended art classes, taking part in many professional events in both artistic and athletic fields.

He stands out for his particular drawing and painting style that coordinates forms, sizes and colours in an innovative way, inspired by the colorful underwater world and the rhythm of the tides. He is now regarded as one of the most influential street artists in Morocco, and that, since 2012. With that same number 12 on his shirt, he's been crowned as a bodysurf national champion.
