Zaamom, born in the ninth in the year 1987 in Morocco, Inspired by art from a young age, he grew up in the Ocean District, This made seawater ink for his drawings and waves a source of inspiration for his works. After studying contemporary literature, Zaamom attended art classes. He has also taken part in many professional artistic and athletic events. He stands out for a particular style that coordinates, sizes and colours in an innovative way, inspired by the abyss, He is now regarded as one of the most influent street artists in Morocco since 2012. And with the same number 12, he's a Moroccan champion in the sport of body surfing.
